Lemon-Blackberry Sparkling Lambrusco and GinLemon-Blackberry Sparkling Lambrusco and Gin

Infused gin with blackberries and thyme, then combine it with Lambrusco and bubbles for a sparkling sipper worth celebrating.

Submitted by Juliana Hale | Published on November 18, 2024

Prep Time:

5 mins

Cook Time:

5 mins

Stand Time:

1 day

Total Time:

10 min

Servings:

6

Ingredients

  • ice
  • 1 ounce Blackberry Gin (see below)
  • 1/2 ounce Simple Syrup (see below)
  • 1/2 ounce lemon juice
  • 3 ounces chilled Lambrusco
  • 3 ounces club soda
  • blackberries and/or a thyme sprig, for garnish

Directions

  1. Fill a wineglass half full with ice. Add Blackberry Gin, Simple Syrup, and lemon juice. Top with Lambrusco and club soda. Garnish with blackberries and/or a thyme sprig.
  2. Blackberry Gin:
    • Combine 1 cup gin, 3/4 cup blackberries, and 1 sprig fresh thyme in a canning jar. Screw on lid and shake. Let stand in a cool, dark place for 1 to 3 days to infuse, shaking once or twice a day. Taste each day to determine desired flavor intensity. Strain over a small bowl. Discard solids and return to jar. Chill, covered, up to 3 months. (Makes 1 cup.)
  3. Simple Syrup:
    1. Heat 1/3 cup each water and sugar in a small saucepan over medium heat, stirring until sugar dissolves and mixture thickens, 3 to 5 minutes. Transfer to a canning jar. Chill, covered, for 1 hour, or up to 1 month. (Makes 1/2 cup.)
